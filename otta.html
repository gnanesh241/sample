ODDANCHATRAM

Oddanchatram is popular for Gandhi Vegetable market, which is one of the largest vegetable market in Tamilnadu.Oddanchatram. Oddanchatram is a town in Dindigul district in the Indian state of Tamil Nadu. It is the second largest town in Dindigul district. Oddanchatram is also famous for vegetable and cattle market. It is located at the base of the western ghats in South India.Oddanchatram was constituted as a second grade Town Panchayat in the year 1959 as per Panchayat Inspector Roc.No. 44621/58 date: 01.04. 1959. It was upgraded to 1st grade Town Panchayat in the year 1964 as per the Panchayat Inspector Roc.
